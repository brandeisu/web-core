The Club Support committee is comprised of student Senate and non-Senate members dedicated to assisting clubs through the club formation process. Club Support is also happy to assist club leaders whenever they have a problem related to continuing to run their clubs. Any questions related to club money or finances should be sent to clubfinance@brandeis.edu. Other emails can be sent to this year's Club Support committee chair, David Herbstritt. Feel free to look at the documents below as a guide for starting a club or maintaining a club, and please fill out and hand in an anti-hazing and club renewal form if your club has yet to do so. Other information and forms can be found at http://www.brandeis.edu/clubs/.
